Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

ConferenceKit is a client-side web application for conference networking. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we access, how it's used, and how it's stored.

Data Storage

All contact data you import into ConferenceKit is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. No data is sent to or stored on any external server. If you clear your browser data, your ConferenceKit data will be deleted.

Google Sign-In

ConferenceKit offers optional Google Sign-In to let you import contacts from your own Google Sheets. When you sign in, we request the following permissions:

  • Google Sheets (read-only) — to read spreadsheet data you choose to import
  • Google Drive (metadata, read-only) — to list your spreadsheets so you can select one to import

We do not modify, delete, or share your Google data. Your Google access token is stored only in your browser's session storage and is automatically cleared when you close the tab.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not use cookies (other than what Google Identity Services requires)
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not have a backend server or database

Third-Party Services

ConferenceKit uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Identity Services — for optional OAuth sign-in
  • Google Sheets API & Google Drive API — to read your spreadsheets when signed in
  • SheetJS (CDN) — to parse .xlsx files locally in your browser

No contact data is transmitted to any of these services. SheetJS runs entirely in your browser.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. All data remains in your browser.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your ConferenceKit data at any time by clearing your browser's site data, or by using the delete options within the app. To revoke Google access, visit your Google Account permissions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
